Empowering the Nation

Rooted in Growth

Since 2022, we've been providing professional skills training for domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg, helping hundreds upskill themselves for better employment opportunities and entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

Founded by Precious Radebe, Empowering the Nation provides skills training for domestic workers and gardeners who were never given the chance to upskill themselves or follow formal educational qualifications. Our training makes participants more marketable when seeking employment and enables them to earn higher wages. They can also become entrepreneurs and start their own small businesses using these newly obtained skills.
