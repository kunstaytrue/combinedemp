Courses

At Empowering the Nation, we offer a range of practical, skills-based training programs designed to help you build confidence, gain experience, and open doors to employment opportunities. Our courses range from short six-week programs to comprehensive six-month qualifications, allowing you to learn at your own pace and according to your career goals.

Six-Month Courses

Our six-month courses are designed to provide in-depth training and hands-on experience in various fields. These programs equip learners with both technical skills and essential life skills to prepare them for long-term success in the workplace or self-employment.

Available Courses:
  • First Aid: Learn essential emergency response techniques to provide care and support in critical situations.
  • Sewing: Develop practical sewing and tailoring skills for personal projects or small business opportunities.
  • Landscaping: Gain experience in garden design, plant care, and outdoor maintenance techniques.
  • Life Skills: Build communication, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities to thrive in both personal and professional settings.

These courses are ideal for individuals seeking career development and comprehensive, structured learning.

Six-Week Courses

Our six-week courses are short, focused programs designed to teach practical skills quickly and effectively. Perfect for anyone looking to upskill, try something new, or start working right away.

Available Courses:
  • Childminding: Learn how to provide safe, engaging, and responsible care for children of various ages.
  • Cooking: Master the basics of meal preparation, kitchen safety, and food presentation.
  • Garden Maintenance: Understand the essentials of lawn care, planting, and outdoor upkeep.

These short-term courses are perfect for quick skill development and entry-level job opportunities in their respective fields.
